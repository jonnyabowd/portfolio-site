Wikibuy Mobile App Redesign

Problem

Capital One acquired Wikibuy in fall 2018 and I was brought on to lead the design team while working to integrate and improve the company’s product offering. The existing Wikibuy mobile application had issues with usability, stability, new user attrition, and a lack of overall revenue generation for our business. My design team and I were tasked with shoring up the customer experience, improving overall engagement, and increasing downstream effects to potential revenue.

My Role

  • Design Management
  • Research
  • Wireframing
  • UX/UI Design
  • Prototyping
  • Design System Development
I worked closely with our product and research team to identify the largest opportunities for improving the overall customer experience and increase mobile purchases through our app. I led my design team into creating a series of viable solutions for improved core user flows and net new functionality that could be implemented on shortened delivery timelines with our mobile engineering team.

Solution

After conducting a series of interviews with our customers, analyzing app usage, and examining performance data we identified the largest pain points and opportunities to improve the overall experience. We then redesigned a set of core flows within the app including a completely new personalized home page, improved search experience, new onboarding flow with push notification enrollment, and a redesigned price comparison experience.

We also designed and built a new in-app shopping flow that allowed us to integrate our flagship Automatic Coupon service. This gave the app full feature parity with our core desktop browser extension product and gave customers on average 30% more savings when shopping through the app.

Results

  • Our new homepage design led to a 25% increase in purchases year-over-year over the previous design.
  • The redesigned price comparison experience increased purchases by around 40% year-over-year.
  • The app store rating increased from 3.3 stars before the redesign to 4.7 stars after core experience enhancements were completed.
  • We successfully rebranded from Wikibuy to the new Capital One Shopping brand in time for a national advertising launch in Fall 2020.

After

Opportunities

  • Generic content and offers meant all customers would see the same experience regardless of preferences or shopping habits.
  • Accessibility issues such as a low contrast color palette and missing field labels prevented users with color blindness and cognitive impairments from using the app.
  • After a series of contextual interviews, we discovered broken or missing core features such as reliable product and store search and base-level account management which caused customers to lose confidence in this shopping app.

Highlights

  • Designed and released a new tile-based content format enabling customers to browse content more easily.
  • Prioritized personalized offers, deals, and tiles to make the experience more tailored to the customer’s shopping preferences while also including a layer of discoverability though features like “Similar Stores” and related product suggestions.
  • Fixed accessibility issues and improved usability allowing customers with color blindness and cognitive impairments to successfully navigate the experience.

Homepage Tile Concepts

My team and I created over 60 unique homepage tile concepts across 5 main product requirements.

Rebrand Guidelines

I developed brand migration guidelines to be used by multiple development, marketing, and agency teams during the rebrand of the Wikibuy platform to Capital One Shopping.

In-App Shopping Experience

My team and I designed a new custom webview shopping experience that kept users within the app in order to provide additional tools while they shopped, helping them save money and find the best deals.

Automatic Coupons At Checkout

One of the primary benefits of building out the in-app webview for shopping trips is that we can programatically access the page content and perform tasks on behalf of the user including checking prices while they shop and automatically applying coupon codes during checkout.
